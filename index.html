Pune Tasty Food

Misal Pav

Misal pav is a popular dish. It consists of misal (a spicy curry usually made from moth beans) and pav (a type of Indian bread roll). The final dish is topped with farsan or sev, onions, lemon and coriander.

Bakarwadi

Since Chitale Bandhu Bakarwadi Spring Roll is an origin of Maharasthra, it is very famous and known for its 'Bakarwadi Spring Roll'.

Kalyan Bhel

Kalyan Bhel House is a renowned chaat and snack house operating since the last 30 years in Pune. Rameshbhau in a short span of time expanded this chaat and snack house business from a street vendor to acquiring 7-8 snack places in and around Pune



Irani Cafe Bun Maska

The Irani Cafe is one of the most popular Irani restaurant in Pune. They are serving all types of Irani cusines and drinks. Ardeshir's Lime Soda and other Flavoured Soda was one of the best thing I found here

Pav Bhaji

Pav bhaji is a fast food dish from India consisting of a thick vegetable curry served with a soft bread roll.

Vada pav

Vada pav, is a vegetarian fast food dish native to the state of Maharashtra. The dish consists of a deep fried potato dumpling placed inside a bread bun sliced almost in half through the middle. It is generally accompanied with one or more chutneys and a green chili pepper.

Pune Tourist Attraction

Shaniwar Wada

Shaniwarwada is a historical fortification. Built in 1732, it was the great seat of the Peshwas of the Maratha Empire

Shreemant Dagdusheth Halwai

Shreemant Dagadusheth Halwai Ganapati Temple in Pune is dedicated to the Hindu God Ganesh. The temple is popular in Maharashtra and is visited by over hundred thousand pilgrims every year.

Sinhgad Fort

The Sinhagad Fort was initially known as "Kondhana" as one of the most famous battles on Sinhgad was fought by Tanaji Malusare, general of Shivaji of the Maratha Empire in order to recapture the fort on March 1670



Raigad Fort

Raigad is a hill fort situated in Mahad, Raigad district of Maharashtra, India. It is one of the strongest fortresses on the Deccan Plateau. It was built by Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj and later made his capital.

Palkhi

Pandharpur Wari, is an annual pilgrimage to the town of Pandharpur, which is the seat of Hindu God Vithoba, in honour of the deity

Lonavala City

Lonavala is a hill station surrounded by green valleys in western India near Mumbai. The Karla Caves and the Bhaja Caves are ancient Buddhist shrines carved out of the rock. They feature massive pillars and intricate relief sculptures.
